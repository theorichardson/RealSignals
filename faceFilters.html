Anime face filter


This is footage of the Anime Style face filter on Snapchat. This algorithm detects facial features and recreates them in an anime style.

This filter, among others, speaks to the future of human interaction and expression in an increasingly digital world. When mixed reality becomes commonplace through widely available MR glasses, the nature of what is 'real' will be transformed.

The plastic surgery of the future will be digital.
