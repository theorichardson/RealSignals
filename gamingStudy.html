Time, Money and Video Games


This new study from Simon-Kucher & Partners speaks to the impact of the pandemic on a number of behaviours relating to video games. The study suggests that COVID-19 led to a 39 percent monthly increase in video game spending and a 19 percent shift from casual gaming to more regular, serious gaming among gamers.

Video gaming is a growing industry that will continue to grow to reach new audiences in the future.
