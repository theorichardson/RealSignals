Cyberpunk 2077 on Google Stadia


After a botched launch on PS4 and Xbox One due to major bugs, glitches and crashes, Cyberpunk 2077 is optimally playable on high-end PC... and Google Stadia. Stadia, having launched similarly to Cyberpunk with bugs and a lackluster technical performance, has improved enough to run the behemoth of a game over a steady, relatively high speed internet connection. This is the first distinct win for cloud gaming that got skeptics like me in the door. The cloud is the future!
